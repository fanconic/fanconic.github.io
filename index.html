M.Sc. Information Technology and Electrical Engineering, ETH Zürich


Hello, I'm Claudio! Until recently, I was a Master's student at ETH Zürich studying Information Technology and Electrical Engineering with a focus on Machine Learning (ML). I wrote my master's thesis at the Boussard Lab at Stanford in biomedical informatics on predictive uncertainty and natural language processing to identify patients at risk of acute care use. I am passionate about (i) the application of ML in a highly sensitive environment such as medicine, (ii) uncertainty estimation with Bayesian models, and (iii) the interpretability of modern deep learning architectures. I have also worked on projects involving variational networks for image registration, multitask learning for autonomous driving and risk prediction based on BERT models.

Before starting my Master's, I interned as a machine learning engineer at IBM and as a management consultant at McKinsey & Company. Previously, I completed my B.Sc. in Electrical Engineering and Information Technology, also at ETH Zürich.
